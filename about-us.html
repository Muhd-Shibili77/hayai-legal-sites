About Hayai

Transforming field service operations with smart, scalable software.

Our Story & Why We Exist

The traditional service management industry is broken. Customers wait hours for engineers, and businesses struggle with paperwork and poor scheduling. Hayai was born out of the necessity to bring order to this chaos.

We built a platform that handles everything from the moment a customer logs a complaint, to the engineer's real-time routing, all the way to final invoicing and AMC tracking.

Our Vision

To become the global standard operating system for field service and complaint management businesses.

Our Mission

To empower service businesses with technology that increases engineer efficiency by 40% while delivering exceptional transparency to end-customers.

Our Core Values

Velocity (Hayai)

Reliability

When appliances break down, people panic. We provide a stable, transparent platform that builds trust.

Smart Innovation

From AI-based route optimization for engineers to predictive AMC scheduling, we let data do the heavy lifting.
