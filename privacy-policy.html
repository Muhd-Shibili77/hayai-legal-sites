Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Hayai. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy applies to all information collected through our Customer Mobile App, Engineer Mobile App, Service Management Platform, and Future Customer Web Portal.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the App or Web Portal, express an interest in obtaining information about us, or otherwise contact us.

  • Personal Information: Names, phone numbers, email addresses, and billing addresses.
  • Device Information: We may collect hardware models, operating systems, and app versions for diagnostics.
  • Location Information: For Engineer apps, we track GPS location to assign nearby tickets. For Customer apps, location is used to dispatch engineers accurately.
  • Uploaded Documents & Images: Images of defective products, engineer proof-of-work photos, and AMC contracts.
  • Complaint & Service Data: Detailed history of your service requests, chats with engineers, and payment history.

3. How We Use Information

We process your information for purposes based on legitimate business interests, the fulfillment of our contract with you, compliance with our legal obligations, and/or your consent.

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • To manage customer complaints and dispatch service engineers.
  • To process payments for repairs and AMC services.
  • To improve the quality of our SaaS platform.

4. Data Sharing & Security

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services (like sharing your address with an assigned engineer), or to fulfill business obligations. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process.

5. Data Retention & User Rights

We will only keep your personal information for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy. You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under 18 years of age. By using the platform, you represent that you are at least 18.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this notice, you may email us at privacy@hayai.com.
